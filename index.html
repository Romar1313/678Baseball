Omar Daniels founder and owner
I am a proud graduate of George Walton Academy located in Monroe, Georgia and during my tenure at GWA I played shortstop and a little pitching. I played there from 2013 - 2019 from middle to high school. After graduation, I accepted a scholarship at Georgia Highlands College (JUCO) to play two years and then transferred to University of Dayton in Dayton, OH. The flyers as we are best known was from 2022 - 2024 were I play 2nd, short and 3rd base. I specialize in infield training.

This location is available for team practices and private lessons. We aim to teach and enhance skills that will help any player improve his/her game. We focus on proper techniques, restore the love of the game, mentally work through challenges and having fun doing what you love. The facility is 15,000 sq feet that comprises of live and machine pitching, space to practice fielding, pitching and hitting techniques.
